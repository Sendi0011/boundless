# Boundless Wins Stellar Community Fund #40 🚀

### What This Means, Why It Matters, and What's Coming Next

We've got big news. **Boundless has officially been awarded in Stellar Community Fund (SCF) #40** 🎉

And honestly? This is a huge milestone for us.

Being selected by the **[@buildonstellar](https://x.com/BuildOnStellar)** and **[@stellarorg](https://x.com/StellarOrg)** teams isn't just about funding — it's validation. It's proof that the problem we're solving matters, and that the way we're solving it on Stellar makes sense.

---

## Why Boundless Exists

Web3 funding is broken. Builders struggle to access capital. Backers struggle to trust outcomes. Communities struggle to coordinate grants, hackathons, bounties, and contributions — all across fragmented tools.

Boundless was created to fix that. We're building **a decentralized funding and contribution platform on Stellar** that brings:

- Transparent, milestone-based funding
- On-chain accountability via Soroban smart contracts
- Community validation before funds move
- A single place to manage **crowdfunding, grants, hackathons, bounties, and contributor rewards**

No guesswork. No "trust me bro." Just verifiable progress.

---

## Why Stellar (and Why SCF #40 Matters)

We chose Stellar for a reason.

- Fast and low-cost transactions
- Real-world usability
- Growing Soroban smart contract ecosystem
- Strong focus on builders and open infrastructure

SCF #40 backing means Boundless is now part of a cohort building **real utility**, not just hype. It gives us the runway to move faster, build deeper, and ship production-ready tooling for the ecosystem.

---

## What We're Building Next

With SCF support, we're focused on delivering:

- **Milestone-based funding contracts** powered by Soroban
- **Contribution Hub** for bounties, RFPs, and OSS work
- **Wallet-linked contributor profiles** with reputation and history
- **KYC + on-ramp/off-ramp integrations** for real-world usage
- **Support for XLM and project tokens** for payouts
- **Hackathon and grant tooling** for communities and orgs

This is infrastructure for builders, backers, and ecosystems — not just another platform.
